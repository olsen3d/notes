About Us:

Pulse Analytics is an innovative healthcare tech company; we develop various web-based decision-support applications to help our clients better understand the complex landscape of oncology and special therapeutics. Our dashboards translate and visualize data to create engaging and useful platforms for our users.

We're focused on sharing the company's vision and guiding our team towards the end goal. We embrace all ideas and hold multiple brainstorming sessions around new themes to progress future projects. Our products are used by top pharmaceutical companies to help them make better market access decisions within the oncology and special therapeutics space.
